Tickets

The ticket prices below are the 3 different kinds of tickets. The one on the left is the normal ticket where you just get to go inside of the haunted mansion inside closed paths. The ticket on the right below is the ticket where you get an tour around telling you the history of the mansion. The third ticket below on the right is all access pass for everything.

Ticket prices:

Normal Ticket

Access to the haunted mansion inside closed paths.

Price: $30

Tour Ticket

Guided tour with the history of the mansion.

Price: $50

All Access Pass

Access to everything the mansion offers.

Price: $75
